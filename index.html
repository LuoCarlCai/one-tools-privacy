Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 17, 2026

One Tools ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the One Tools mobile application.

Information We Collect

One Tools is designed to provide utility features such as calculator, unit conversion, QR code generation and scanning, image watermarking, image compression, and speech-to-text. We do not require you to create an account to use the app.

Depending on which feature you use, the app may request access to the following device permissions:

  • Camera: used only for scanning QR codes.
  • Microphone / Speech Recognition: used only for speech-to-text features.
  • Photos: used only when you choose images from your library or save edited images back to your device.

We do not collect personal information unless it is required to provide a feature you choose to use.

How Your Information Is Used

Information accessed through device permissions is used only to provide the requested app functionality. For example, camera access is used for QR scanning, and microphone or speech recognition access is used for transcription.

Data Storage

Some app content, such as recent history or preferences, may be stored locally on your device to improve your experience. We do not sell your personal data.

Third-Party Services

The app may use Apple-provided services such as in-app purchases and system-level speech recognition APIs. These services are governed by Apple's own terms and privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

One Tools is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Choices

You can manage app permissions at any time in your device Settings. If you deny certain permissions, some features may not function properly.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
